About

From AI advice to working systems

Grai Labs combines strategic AI consulting with hands-on system development.

We help organisations decide what to build, design the right evaluation approach, develop the underlying models or workflows, and test whether a system is useful, reliable and ready for real-world adoption.

Our work is practical and evidence-led. We believe AI systems should be useful, testable, understandable and responsibly adopted inside the organisations that use them.

Method

How we work

A five-stage process — because sequence matters as much as substance.

01
Discover

We identify where AI can create real value and where it is likely to fail.

02
Design

We shape the workflow, data requirements, evaluation criteria and human oversight model.

03
Build

We develop prototypes, models, automations and interfaces that fit the organisation's actual work.

04
Evaluate

We test usefulness, reliability, risks and limitations before scaling.

05
Adopt

We help teams understand, govern and improve the system over time.
